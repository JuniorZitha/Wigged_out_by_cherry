Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram, or fill out the contact form above. We'll guide you through the selection process and help you choose the perfect wig for your needs.

Do you offer custom colors?

Yes! We offer custom colors and HD lace options on preorder. Contact us to discuss your preferences and we'll create a custom piece just for you.

How long do the wigs last?

With proper care, our premium Brazilian wigs can last 1-2 years or more. We provide care instructions with every purchase to help you maintain your wig's beauty.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities across South Africa. Delivery times and costs vary by location. Contact us for specific delivery information.
